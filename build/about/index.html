Types of work:

Visual identity

We create design packages including logotype, colourpalete, typeface etc, and the applications needed for the purpose. See Åhnberg & Partners

Type design

We custom design typefaces, taking them all the way from sketches to working in the text editor. See Linder unlimited

Workshops

We occasionally lecture in design and typography at art universities and other places. See Rosengård

Art & research

We do independent art work and reserach projects along with our commercial practice. See Queer Type

Art direction

Choosing visua langugage/imagery, styling and over seeing production to make sure the concept is maintained throughout all units. See JiNilson/SonyMusic

Exhibition design

Spatial large scale graphics, display solutions for public spaces, wayfinding and signage. See Seccesion Gallery/Emily Roysdon

Editorial design

Book cover art, bookdesign, magazine design. Contact with printers and book binders. See Art Workers
